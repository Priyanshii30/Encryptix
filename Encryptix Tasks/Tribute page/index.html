About Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdin Abdul Kalam, known as the Missile Man of India, was the 11th President of India. He was a scientist and an inspirational leader who made significant contributions to India's space and missile programs. Dr. Kalam was loved by millions for his humility, wisdom, and dedication to the nation.
